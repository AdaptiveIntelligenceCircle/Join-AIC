# Code of Conduct (short)

We adhere to a respectful and safe environment.

## Community Commitment
- Respect all members.

- Harassment or discrimination is not tolerated.

- Provide constructive feedback.

## How to report
Email `security@yourdomain` or open a private issue (use Maintainers contact). Reports will be handled confidentially.

## Actions
Violations may result in: warning, removal from team, block from repo.
