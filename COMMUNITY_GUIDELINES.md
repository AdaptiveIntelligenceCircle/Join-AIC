# Community Guidelines — AIC

Goal: Maintain a safe, respectful, and creative environment.

1. **Respectful & Professional**
- No personal attacks, no insults.

- Focus on code, ideas, no personal attacks.

2. **Transparent when necessary**
- When presenting designs or threat models, separate `sensitive` parts (no public keys, PII).

3. **Discourage malicious behavior**
- Any code/patch that can be used for harm will be rejected without controls, ethical mitigation.

4. **Criticism is necessary but constructive**
- Comment clearly, provide evidence, avoid ad-hominem.

5. **Right to opt-in**
- Node independence is respected; no pressure to join Organization or governance.

6. **Liability**
- Contributors are responsible for the license of their contributions.

Violation: Maintainers can warn → close issue/PR → revoke privileges until resolved.
